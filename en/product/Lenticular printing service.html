Lenticular printing service

Update: 2018-09-28 15:19      View:
 

Compared with that of Photoshop,the interface of PSDTO3D99 is relatively simple and easy to understand,its profession makes its performance the top among others.
The interface of the PSDTO3D99 software is clear and simplified, but it is highly professional and practical. Besides an ideal stereo effect, it can also make special effect on the stereograph, such as double changes, multi-variation, rotating and the scaling etc. By study and research, you can also make more attractive effects.
 PSDTO3D99 software application instructions
The software is used in making stereographs and variation&rotating images with PSD layer files, it not only has overall functions, simple and stable operation, quick formation, but also even monolayer can produce stereo sense. It can produce single effect or all at the same time below:
1. Layer stereo: produce stereo effect by layer shifting.
2. The simulation of stereo: through a line to change a single layer into stereo.
Production steps:
1. File--input PSD documents (the resolution graph must be 150 pixels/inch, RGB or CMYK mode). Note: if the picture is too small, resolution can be higher, for example: make a picture of 30 * 40; resolution can be set 200 pixels/inch.
2. Set software parameters:
Grating: In the software direct input grating data would have some difference with the accurate one. Here adjustment can be avoided. (Make the stereo effect by using the accurate one after outputting), of course, you can also input a ballpark figure according to the Lpi of grating you want. For example: 31.75.
Lens: the average is 10 lens (equivalent to the numbers of photos ,if it is the serial photos of a stereo photography equipment , that would be the number of photos) ,you can also use  more than 10, but if the specification of picture is excessive ,it will course abnormality of the software. If the number of lens is too small, it will weaken the stereo effect.
Focus: the layer of a picture’s main body
Depth of focus: it can be amplified, such as if the parallax is over big after delineating lines and shifting, then decreases depth of focus ( excessive parallax would cause vagueness and blurring). Note: here can also set the single layer depth of focus. If depth of focus is big ,the shifting data is small, then if small depth of focus ,big shifting data.
3.Shifting: frontal scene positive, background negative, according to preview effect after the preview (with the red /blue glasses to watch) and parallax ,adjust the data to the rational one (preview effect is the final effect.)
4. Monolayer delineating:
A. Set the “display” as “monolayer”, and then monolayer image appears.
B. Select the layer need to delineate lines, click “save file” in the toolbar, toolbar on the right side of the software jump to monolayer delineat the perspective relations of the object. Form a reasonable scope from the starting point to the end. Double click to make an enclosing.
D. Click preview to watch the effect of delineating lines. That a strong stereo effect with no deformation would be the ideal case. Slider in the “focus” column can modulate monolayer depth of focus and parallax.
E. Back to "multi-layer Setting" interface.
F. Display all the pictures, preview and watch the integral 3D effect.
5. Save the file: delineating lines finished or unfinished can all be saved for backup.
